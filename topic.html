What is Sonar System?

  • Sonar system is a type of system that can be used for a certain type of transmitter called "Piezoelectric Transducer". This transducer has the property of an electric power supply that creates sound of waves and vibration when it is actived. It is also used as a propagation to navigate and communicate with other objects that is under water or beneath the surface.
